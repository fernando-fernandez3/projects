## Why GitFam?

### For Families
- **Own your data** - No subscriptions, no corporate servers, no lock-in
- **Future-proof** - Built on simple, open standards (Markdown, MP4, Git)
- **Redundant by design** - Clone to multiple locations, never lose a memory
- **Collaborative** - Family members can contribute from anywhere
- **Private or public** - You decide who has access

### For Future Generations
- **Authentic voices** - Video interviews preserve personality, not just facts
- **Context and wisdom** - Understand not just what happened, but why it mattered
- **Family identity** - Know where you come from and what values you inherit
- **Living history** - Stories that textbooks and databases can never capture

## Project Philosophy

GitFam is designed to be:
1. **Simple** - No databases, no complex software. Just files and folders.
2. **Accessible** - Technical and non-technical family members can contribute
3. **Replicable** - Any family can fork this and make it their own
4. **Long-lasting** - Built to survive decades, not dependent on any company
